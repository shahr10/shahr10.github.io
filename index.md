### Aerospace Engineer | GNC | Cislunar Astrodynamics | Quantum Communication (QKD)

Aerospace engineer and researcher working at the intersection of **flight dynamics, guidance and control, astrodynamics, and quantum communication systems**. ## About

I am a graduate researcher in Aerospace Engineering at **Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University**, currently affiliated with the **Explainable Intelligence for Space (XD) Lab**. My research spans **cislunar orbital dynamics**, **quantum key distribution (QKD) performance analysis**, and **safe autonomous systems**, with applications to next-generation space communication and navigation architectures.

I have experience across **academic research and aerospace industry internships**, and I am particularly interested in problems that require **end-to-end systems thinking**, from theoretical modeling to mission-level feasibility.
